Sector B: Environment Prevention & Pollution Control Measures

Statutory Reference: Rule 13(1)A(b)

Scope: Installation of effluent treatment plants, prevention of contamination of streams, ponds, lakes, and groundwater, abatement of air and dust pollution, mine drainage systems, environmental monitoring, and reclamation of abandoned mines.
