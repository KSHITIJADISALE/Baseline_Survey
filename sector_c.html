Sector C: Health Care

Statutory Reference: Rule 13(1)A(c)

Scope: Establishment and upgradation of primary and secondary health facilities, deployment of physicians and paramedical staff, procurement of diagnostic equipment, operation of mobile health units, and provision of group health insurance for mine-affected persons.
