Sector D: Education

Statutory Reference: Rule 13(1)A(d)

Scope: Construction of school/college/vocational training institute buildings, additional classrooms, laboratories, libraries, art and craft rooms, toilet blocks, drinking water provisions, residential schools, residential hostels for students of affected areas and teachers in remote areas, sports infrastructure, engagement of teachers/other supporting staff, e-learning setup, other arrangements of transport facilities (bus/van/cycles/rickshaws, etc.), and nutrition-related programs. Financial support to students of affected areas for pursuing education in Government/Government-aided institutions of higher education.
