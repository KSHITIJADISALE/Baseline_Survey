H-III

Toilet Coverage & Open Declaration Free (ODF) Status

▼
Item No. Survey Question Data Format Field Entry
H-III.1 What is the Open Declaration Free (ODF) status of the village? ODF/ODF+/ODF++/Non-ODF
ODF (Open Defecation Free): This means no visible feces are found in the environment, and all households and public institutions use safe, operational toilets.
ODF+ (Open Defecation Free Plus): This builds on ODF by ensuring that all community and public toilets are functional, well-maintained, and that there are effective systems in place for solid and liquid waste management.
ODF++ (Open Defecation Free Plus-Plus): This is the highest level, requiring that all septage and sewage are safely managed, treated, and disposed of. It strictly prohibits the dumping of untreated waste into open drains or natural water bodies.
Non-ODF (Non-Open Defecation Free): Not included, not declared, or not covered under the Open Dedication framework.

H-III.4 How many more toilets are required? Count required
